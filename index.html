About Me

Hello! I'm Taher, a second year Computer Science student at the University of British Columbia in Vancouver.


As a child, I always enjoyed toying with computers and customizing software to look cool or provide some extra functionality. This was never anything complicated, mostly just playing around with internal settings or jailbreaking my iOS devices, but technology always intrugured me and finding ways to make it different or better was my favourite part.


Through personal projects and a startup I co-founded (https://www.getdime.ca/), I've gained experience in mobile development, full stack web development and certain aspects of product management. I'm always learning and I'm currently exploring the field of Machine Learning and its applications.


Outside of work I enjoy reading, running and talking with friends over food. I also love meeting new people so whether you would like to work together on something or simply grab a coffee, feel free to reach out!
